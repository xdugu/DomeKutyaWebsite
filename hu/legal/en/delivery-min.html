The delivery of the packages is done by Magyar Posta MPL company in the area of Hungary. Outside Hungary we usually use DPD or GLS to deliver the orders. Our aim is to post the products as soon as possible that is why we choose MPL Express parcel delivery mode.

Average delivery time is 1-2 days for orders to Hungary. International Orders can take 3-5 days

Cost of shipping (within Hungary): 990 Ft. Cost of shipping to EU countries: 3 - 5 euros depending on which country.
Pay on delivery is: 1290 Ft
