The delivery of the packages is done by Magyar Posta MPL company in the area of Hungary. Outside Hungary we usually use DPD or GLS to deliver the orders. Our aim is to post the products as soon as possible that is why we choose MPL Express parcel delivery mode.

Average delivery time is 1-2 days for orders to Hungary. Internation Orders can take 3-5 days

Cost of shipping (within Hungary): 1800 Ft. Cost of shipping to EU countries: 18-25 euros depending on which country

Pay on delivery (in Hungary): 700ft extra

The shipping cost of the collars and leashes is 450 Ft, in case of paying on delivery it is 750 Ft. They are post as letters. In case of paying on delivery we charge an extra 300 Ft above the shipping cost.
